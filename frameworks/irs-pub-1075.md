# IRS Publication 1075 (PUB 1075)

## What It Is
Security and privacy requirements for protecting **Federal Tax Information (FTI)**—applies to agencies, contractors, and MSPs handling FTI.

## Why It Matters
- Mandatory safeguards for FTI environments  
- Strong alignment with **NIST SP 800‑53** controls  
- Clear expectations for access, logging, encryption, IR

## Core Requirements (examples)
- **Access Control**: least privilege, MFA  
- **Audit Logging**: log access to FTI; retain as required  
- **Encryption**: FIPS‑validated crypto in transit/at rest  
- **Incident Response**: defined timelines and notifications  
- **Physical Security**: controlled facilities and logging

## How to Use It (Practical)
- Run a **gap assessment** against PUB 1075 requirements  
- Map to existing **NIST/ISO** controls to avoid duplication  
- Bake requirements into **IR runbooks** and onboarding
