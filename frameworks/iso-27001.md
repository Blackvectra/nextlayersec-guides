# ISO/IEC 27001

## What It Is
The international standard for building an **Information Security Management System (ISMS)**—governance, risk, and controls across people, process, and tech.

## Why It Matters
- **Globally recognized** for contracts and vendor trust
- **Risk‑based** approach that scales
- **Bridges** business and technical security

## Core Elements
- Leadership & governance
- Risk assessment & treatment
- Policies & controls (Annex A groups)
- Continuous improvement of the ISMS

## How to Use It (Practical)
- Use Annex A domains to **structure policies**
- Tie risk register items to **specific controls**
- Revisit **at least annually** or after major change
