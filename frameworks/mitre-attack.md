# MITRE ATT&CK

## What It Is
A living knowledge base of **adversary tactics and techniques** observed in the wild, organized by kill‑chain stage (tactics).

## Why It Matters
- **Operational**: maps alerts and hunts to real attacker behavior  
- **Measurable**: shows coverage/gaps across tactics/techniques  
- **Reusable**: supports purple‑team tests and reporting

## How to Use It (Practical)
- **Tag** alerts/incidents with ATT&CK techniques (e.g., T1059 PowerShell)  
- **Plan** hunts around risky tactics (Initial Access, Privilege Escalation)  
- **Test** detections by emulating relevant techniques  
- **Report** incidents using ATT&CK terms to standardize comms

## Example
**PrintNightmare**: Exploitation aligns with **T1068 (Exploitation for Privilege Escalation)**; add detections for suspicious spooler activity.
