# SOC 2 (AICPA Trust Services Criteria)

## What It Is
An **assurance framework** for service organizations. Independent auditors assess controls against five Trust Services Criteria (TSC).

## Why It Matters
- Common **customer requirement** for SaaS/MSP providers  
- Focus on **operational effectiveness** (Type II)  
- Builds **trust** and due‑diligence readiness

## Trust Services Criteria
- **Security**, **Availability**, **Processing Integrity**, **Confidentiality**, **Privacy**

## How to Use It (Practical)
- Map policies/controls to relevant TSCs  
- Track evidence over time for **Type II** reporting  
- Align engineering + operations to pass audits cleanly
